Ultimate Pancake Recipe

This Recipe provides the ultimate guide to a mouth watering pancake dish.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tea spoons of salt
  • 1 crate of egg
  • 4 satchets of butter
  • 9 spoons of sugar
  • 30grams of milk

Steps

  1. Bake in preheated oven until puffy and brown, 20 to 30 minutes.
  2. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  3. Spread bacon squares into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Cook bacon under preheated broiler until brown, 7 to 10 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  6. Beat eggs, sugar, and salt together in a bowl; add milk and flour alternately while mixing with a spoon to make a thin batter. Pour batter into the hot baking dish with the bacon.
